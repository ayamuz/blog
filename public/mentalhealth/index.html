How to calm our inner voice

Most of us know how difficult it can be to deal with our inner voice. Although it might be beneficial during some creative situations or while we remember great experiences, it certainly can get in our way during difficult times. It makes us doubt ourselves and can even overtake our emotions. That negative inner voice is known as chatter. While it is easy to let our inner voice take control of our thoughts, different techniques are effective in dealing with this phenomenon.
